How it works

Model proposes. You dispose.

Every path — speaking or tapping — lands on the same plain-language card. Nothing irreversible happens without your yes.

  1. 01

    Speak

    Press and hold one big button and say it in your own words — Hindi, English, or a mix. A local Gemma model turns it into one structured action.

  2. 02

    See

    The screen shows the action in one big sentence — who, what, how much — with the photo of the person you're reaching. No jargon, no menus.

  3. 03

    Confirm

    You say yes, or tap HAAN. Risky things ask twice. Anything can be stopped. Then it returns home — there's nowhere to get lost.

The safety model is the product

Predictability beats capability.

For someone afraid of getting it wrong, knowing exactly what will happen matters more than doing everything. Every action is sorted into a tier — and the riskier it is, the more it asks of you.
